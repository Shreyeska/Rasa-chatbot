About Us

“Naritwa wants to give Nepali women the means they need to know their rights and proudly claim them by making legal facts accessible and giving them connection to important resources. Naritwa is an easy-to-use chatbot designed to simplify complicated legal information about women's rights in Nepal. Our solution provides clear explanations, helpful resources and support services using real-life examples. It focuses on simplifying and providing information anonymously to ensure their privacy at all times. By demystifying legal complexities and providing essential resources, Naritwa aims to empower Nepali women to confidently recognize, understand, and assert their rights.
