Ghimire and Associates

Ghimire & Associates is a leading law firm in Nepal specializing in women's rights and gender equality. With a team of experienced lawyers and legal experts, they provide comprehensive legal services and advocacy for women's rights cases. They have a strong track record of success in advocating for gender equality and empowering Nepali women through legal means.

Empowerment Counseling Center

The Empowerment Counseling Center offers professional counseling services tailored specifically for women in Nepal. Their team of licensed counselors provides support and guidance to women facing various challenges, including domestic violence, discrimination, and legal issues. They offer a safe and confidential environment for women to express themselves and seek assistance in navigating their rights.

Women's Empowerment Nepal (WEN)

Women's Empowerment Nepal (WEN) is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting gender equality and women's empowerment in Nepal. They offer a wide range of programs and services aimed at enhancing women's access to education, healthcare, legal aid, and economic opportunities. WEN collaborates with various stakeholders, including government agencies, NGOs, and grassroots organizations, to advocate for women's rights and create positive social change.
